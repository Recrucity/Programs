Thank you for downloadfing AFKv1!
This program will automatically move a little for you in roblox (probably works in all games) so you don't get AFK Kicked!

This is perfect for games like My Resturant and other games where you have workers working for you.

HOW TO ENABLE THE PROGRAM:
* Open AFK.exe
* Click on roblox
* Enjoy!

HOW TO DISABLE THE PROGRAM:
* Right click the Anti-AFK icon in your taskbar
* Click exit

If you don't find the anti-afk icon in your taskbar:
* Click the arrow button in your task bar
* Right click the anti-afk icon
* Click exit
